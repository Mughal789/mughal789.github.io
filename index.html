About

Building detector workflows for high-energy physics.

I am a PhD experimental physicist with postdoctoral experience in detector R&D, tracking detector operation, reconstruction software, GEANT4 simulation, detector calibration, data quality monitoring, and large-scale scientific data analysis.

My work spans sPHENIX, STAR, NA62, CMS, and ALICE. I have worked on TPC tracking calibration, GTK beam spectrometer commissioning, silicon sensor testing, detector performance validation, Monte Carlo production, and physics analysis software.

Detector, tracking, simulation, and analysis expertise.
